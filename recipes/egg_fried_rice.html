Fried Rice and Egg

A quick and easy yet delicious fried rice and egg recipe.

This recipe is simple and satisfies the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Day old refrigerated rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl, whisk until you see air bubbles and set aside.
  2. Pour oil into a frying pan and set to medium heat.
  3. While we wait for the oil to heat, lightly salt the eggs.
  4. Once oil is hot, pour in your eggs and season with garlic powder.
  5. Once eggs are cooked but slightly runny, set aside.
  6. Pour your rice into the frying pan, spread and flatten the rice to develop a crust on the bottom
  7. Once you have a nice crust on the bottom pour some soy sauce into the rice and stir.
  8. Turn off the heat, set pan aside, add the eggs again and stir.
  9. Garnish with green onion and serve.
