Popcorn Chicken

A delicious protein packed snack if you are craving chicken nuggets

A simple and delicious recipe for some juice chicken nuggets.

Ingredeients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Buttermilk
  • Panko Crumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breast into even, bite-sized pieces.
  2. Whish the flour, salt, and garlic powder together in a medium bowl
  3. Dip the chicken bites into the flour mixture, followed by the buttermilk then panko.
  4. Preheat oil in a deep frying pan to 350 degrees and pop the coated chicken inside. Fry in small batches until golden brown.
  5. Allow the chicken to rest on a paper towl to removes excess oil.
  6. Serve with your favorite sauce!
